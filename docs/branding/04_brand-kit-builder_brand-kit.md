## Section 3: Logo System

### Primary Logo
The Allura logo consists of a distinctive droplet/arch mark combined with the wordmark "allura" in lowercase.

**Logo Mark:**
- Stylized droplet/arch shape with three colored segments
- Represents memory, connection, and human-centered technology
- The arch form suggests shelter, protection, and care (Caregiver archetype)

**Logo Colors:**
- Blue segment: Royal Blue (#2563EB)
- Orange segment: Coral/Orange (#F97316)
- Green segment: Forest Green (#16A34A)

### Logo Usage Rules

#### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the lowercase "a" in "allura" on all sides.

#### Minimum Sizes
| Application | Minimum Size |
|-------------|--------------|
| Digital | 120px width |
| Print | 1.5 inches width |

#### Logo Variants
1. **Primary:** Full logo (mark + wordmark) - preferred for all applications
2. **Horizontal:** For space-constrained layouts
3. **Icon-only:** For favicon, app icons, social avatars
4. **Wordmark-only:** For text-heavy contexts

#### Logo Misuse (Never Do)
- ❌ Stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Rotate the logo
- ❌ Change the logo colors
- ❌ Add drop shadows or effects
- ❌ Place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- ❌ Use the mark without the wordmark in brand contexts

### Co-Branding Rules
When appearing with partner logos:
- Maintain clear space between Allura logo and partner logo
- Allura logo should be equal or larger than partner logos
- Use the monochrome version when color restrictions apply

| **Overline** | 12px / 0.75rem | 600 (SemiBold) | 1.2 | 0.1em |
| **Button** | 16px / 1rem | 600 (SemiBold) | 1 | 0.02em |

### Typography Rules

#### Headings
- Use sentence case (not ALL CAPS)
- Keep headlines concise (max 8 words for H1)
- Use bold weight for emphasis, not italics

#### Body Text
- Optimal line length: 60-75 characters
- Paragraph spacing: 1.5rem (24px) between paragraphs
- Use left-aligned text for readability (avoid justified)

#### Special Text Treatments
- **Tagline:** ALL CAPS, letter-spacing 0.15em, font-weight 500
- **Stats/Numbers:** Use tabular figures where available
- **Labels:** Uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em, font-weight 600

### Web Font Loading Strategy
- Use `font-display: swap` to prevent FOIT (Flash of Invisible Text)
- Preload Inter font files for critical above-fold content
- System font stack as immediate fallback

## Section 6: Visual Language

### Photography Style
- **Subject Matter:** Real people in authentic moments, technology that fades into the background
- **Composition:** Clean, uncluttered, generous whitespace
- **Color Treatment:** Natural colors with slight warmth, avoid heavy filters
- **Mood:** Optimistic, calm, human-centered
- **Avoid:** Stock photos with obvious watermarks, overly posed corporate imagery

### Illustration Style
- **Style:** Abstract geometric shapes with soft curves
- **Elements:** Arch shapes, droplet forms, connected nodes
- **Colors:** Use brand palette with transparency/overlay effects
- **Usage:** Product UI mockups, hero backgrounds, feature illustrations

### Iconography
**Icon Style:** Line icons with 2px stroke weight, rounded caps

**Core Icon Set:**
- Secure (shield)
- Family (people)
- Memories (photo frame)
- Sharing (arrow)
- Timeline (clock)
- Search (magnifying glass)
- Privacy (lock)
- Backup (cloud)
- Connected (link)
- Support (headset)

**Icon Usage:**
- Size: 24px default, 20px for dense UI, 32px for feature highlights
- Color: Inherit from text color or use brand colors for emphasis
- Grid: All icons built on 24x24px grid

### Graphic Patterns
- **Arch Motif:** Repeating arch shapes in brand colors
- **Node Network:** Connected dots representing memory graph
- **Gradient Blobs:** Soft, overlapping color fields
- **Usage:** Backgrounds, section dividers, presentation templates

## Section 7: Voice & Messaging

### Voice Definition

| Dimension | Allura's Position |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Tone** | Warm, approachable, intelligent |
| **Language** | Clear, jargon-free, human |
| **Perspective** | First-person plural ("we") when speaking as brand |
| **Personality** | Helpful guide, not know-it-all expert |

### Voice Spectrum

| Aspect | Range | Allura's Position |
|--------|-------|-------------------|
| **Formality** | Casual ← → Formal | Slightly casual (60%) |
| **Enthusiasm** | Reserved ← → Energetic | Moderately enthusiastic (70%) |
| **Complexity** | Simple ← → Technical | Accessible with depth (50%) |
| **Humor** | Serious ← → Playful | Occasionally warm, never silly (30%) |

### Must-Not List
- ❌ "Revolutionary" or "game-changing" (overused tech clichés)
- ❌ "AI-powered" as the main benefit (focus on outcome, not tech)
- ❌ "Seamless" or "effortless" (implies no work needed)
- ❌ "Best-in-class" or "industry-leading" (unsubstantiated claims)
- ❌ "Leverage" (use "use" instead)
- ❌ "Synergy" or other corporate buzzwords
- ❌ All caps for emphasis (use bold or color instead)
- ❌ Exclamation points (keep calm and confident)

### Headline Standards
- **Length:** 4-8 words for H1, 6-10 words for H2
- **Structure:** Subject + benefit or question + answer
- **CTA Style:** Action-oriented, specific outcome
- **Examples:**
  - ✅ "AI systems don't forget. They remember what matters."
  - ✅ "Memory that shows its work."
  - ✅ "Smarter memory. Stronger systems."

### Body Copy Standards
- **Reading Level:** 8th-10th grade (Flesch-Kincaid)
- **Sentence Length:** Average 15-20 words
- **Paragraph Length:** 2-4 sentences maximum
- **Evidence Requirement:** Support claims with specific data or examples

### Platform-Specific Voice Adjustments

| Platform | Adjustment |
|----------|------------|
| **Website** | Full brand voice, comprehensive messaging |
| **Social Media** | More conversational, emoji-appropriate |
| **Email** | Personal, direct, clear CTAs |
| **Product UI** | Concise, instructional, helpful |
| **Documentation** | Clear, structured, educational |

### Key Messages

**Primary Message:**
"Allura is the sovereign memory layer for AI. Capture, connect and activate what matters—so your AI systems never forget."

**Supporting Messages:**
1. **Human First:** We design for people, not technology.
2. **Built on Trust:** We protect what matters most.
3. **Clarity in Everything:** We make complex things feel simple.
4. **Stronger Together:** We believe in connection, community and impact.

**Tagline Usage:**
- **Primary:** "MEMORY THAT SHOWS ITS WORK"
- **Usage:** Always in ALL CAPS with letter-spacing
- **Placement:** Below logo on cover pages, in email signatures, social bios
