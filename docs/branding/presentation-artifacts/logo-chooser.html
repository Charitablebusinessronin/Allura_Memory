Logo Variants

Primary Wordmark

The default face of allura. The full wordmark with its signature gesture — the gentle upward curve of the final 'a' that creates a subtle, subconscious smile. This is the mark people remember.

Full Mark

The wordmark at its most confident scale. Slightly larger proportions, optimized for hero moments and brand statements. The Full Mark lets the gesture breathe.

Icon Only

The distilled essence of allura — the "a" gesture, isolated and iconic. Compact, recognizable, and instantly warm even at tiny sizes. Best for social avatars, app icons, and favicons.

Alternate Mark

A special-occasion variant for merchandise, editorial layouts, and creative campaigns. Maintains the gesture but opens new possibilities for expression. Use for branded merchandise, experimental campaigns, and partner co-branding.

Usage Rules

Rule Guidance
Clear Space Minimum clear space equals the x-height of the lowercase 'a'. Never crowd the mark.
Minimum Size 120px wide (digital), 1.5 inches (print), 32×32px (favicon / icon only).
Color Lock Use approved brand colors only. Never apply effects, gradients, or filters.
The Gesture Never flatten or alter the upward curve of the final 'a'. It is our smile.
Proportion Never stretch, rotate, or distort. Scale uniformly from center.
